Who we are

The Dram Club unleashes your whisky persona,
celebrating your distinct palate and personality
within the vast world of whisky. It's not just about
the drink; it's about you and your journey through
the world of spirits.

How we Started

The Club was founded by two young professionals -
Vinayak, an Investment manager at a wealth firm,
and Swati Sharma, Marketing lead at a Start-up.
Both have been whisky aficionados for a while
with an interesting story of their tryst with the world of whiskies.
